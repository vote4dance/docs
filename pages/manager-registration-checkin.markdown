# Registration and check-in

## Registration workflows

Use the `Registration` section to control participant intake.

Tabs and purpose:

- `Registrations`: participant entries and status operations
- `Teams`: accepted entries by class
- `Periods`: registration windows, pricing, rules, and form settings
- `Data`: registration-focused import/export utilities

## Registration periods

For each period define:

- Name and start/end timestamps
- Payment mode: free, manual, or online
- Acceptance mode: manual or direct
- Currency and price model
- Rule/form configuration
- Allowed classes

You can reorder periods to control display order.

## Registration operations

In `Registrations`, managers can:

- Filter by class, status, payment, and import state
- Change status in bulk or per-entry
- Email selected participants
- Import approved registrations into competition teams
- Inspect logs and payment details per entry

## Check-in workflows

Use `Checkin` for event-day signoff and cancellation control.

Main controls:

- Configure check-in open/close window
- Track organization-level check-in status
- Mark team attendance/cancellation reasons
- Capture signed check-in submissions
- Apply/unapply check-in to competition state

## Recommended check-in sequence

1. Open check-in window in settings
2. Let organizations mark teams and sign
3. Monitor cancellation report
4. Apply check-in once submissions are complete
5. Unapply only if corrections are required

## Registration and check-in done checklist

1. Registration periods and rules are published correctly
2. Entry statuses are validated
3. Approved entries are imported to teams
4. Check-in window is active at the right time
5. Signed check-ins are applied before live rounds
