# Understanding Vote4Dance

Before you jump into specific workflows, understand how Vote4Dance organizes people, organizations, and events.

## What you'll learn in this section

1. **System Model** — How Federations, Events, Schools/Clubs, and Dancers fit together
2. **Finding Your Role** — Decision tree to identify which role(s) you have
3. **Key Terminology** — Terms you'll see throughout the docs
4. **An Introduction to Manager** — The central control panel for event organizers

After this section, you'll be ready to jump into the Getting Started workflows, confident that you understand which role applies to you and how the pieces fit together.
