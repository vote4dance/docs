# Scrutineer applying for a school or club

Some scrutineers work through a school, club, or other organization rather than only as individuals.

## What this step is for

This step connects the scrutineer to the organization context used for support, permissions, or licensing.

## How it works

1. Request access to the correct organization.
2. Wait for approval where needed.
3. Confirm that the organization relationship appears in the account.
4. Use the right context in later workflows.

## What success looks like

The scrutineer is linked to the right organization for the federation path they use.
