# Scrutineer getting a license

A scrutineer may need a federation-recognized license before taking on official work.

## What this step is for

This step explains how the scrutineer becomes officially eligible for assignment.

## How it works

1. Confirm whether the federation requires a scrutineer license.
2. Apply through the correct organization or federation path.
3. Complete payment if required.
4. Wait for approval if required.
5. Confirm active status before accepting assignments.

## What success looks like

The scrutineer is licensed and ready to work under federation rules.
