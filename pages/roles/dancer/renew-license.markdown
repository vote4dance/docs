# Dancer renew license

Renewal keeps the dancer eligible from one season to the next.

## What this step is for

This step explains how dancers avoid gaps in eligibility when a license expires.

## Where to do it

- direct dancer workflow: [Dancer self-service](/federation-licenses/dancer-self-service/)
- organization-managed workflow: [Organization admin](/federation-licenses/organization-admin/)

## How it works

1. Check the expiration of your current license.
2. Confirm the new season has opened.
3. Choose the correct renewal item or organization-managed path.
4. Complete payment if required.
5. Wait for approval if required.
6. Confirm the renewed license is active before registering for the next season.

## Good practice

- Renew before registration deadlines become urgent.
- Confirm whether your club manages the process for you.
- Re-check the season year and item name carefully.

## Common blockers

- The dancer renews too late.
- The wrong season year is selected.
- The dancer assumes old approval carries into the new season automatically.

## What success looks like

The dancer enters the new season with active eligibility and no licensing gap.

## Best timing

Renew before you need to register for the first event of the new season. Waiting until registration day creates the most avoidable support issues.
