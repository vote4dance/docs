# Dancer applying for a school or club

In Vote4Dance, schools and clubs are generally handled as organizations. Joining the correct one affects licensing, team management, and support.

## What this step is for

This step links the dancer to the organization that represents or supports them.

## Who does this step

The dancer requests or accepts the relationship.

The school or club approves it.

## Before you start

Make sure you know:

- the exact name of the school or club
- whether your dancer license is supposed to be self-service or club-managed
- whether the club must already be approved by the federation for your path

## How it works

1. Find the correct school or club.
2. Request membership or accept an invitation.
3. Wait for approval if the organization uses approval.
4. Confirm the organization appears in your account context.
5. Use that organization in later licensing or registration flows when needed.

## Why it matters

Some federations require organization context for:

- license application
- class participation
- team management
- club representation

## Common blockers

- The dancer joins the wrong club.
- The request stays pending because no admin reviewed it.
- The dancer expects club access to replace federation approval automatically.

## What success looks like

The dancer is linked to the correct school or club and can use that context in later workflows.

## Practical advice

- Join the school or club before event registration week.
- If you dance in more than one context, confirm which club should be used for the next license or event.
- If the wrong club was selected, fix that before asking anyone to create a license or submit a registration.
