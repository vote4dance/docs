# Dancer federation status through school or club

The school or club normally handles federation joining. For the dancer, this step is about making sure that federation status is correctly established through the organization, license, and eligibility setup.

## What this step is for

This step makes sure the dancer is visible in the correct federation context before registration or ranking matters, without implying that the dancer personally joins the federation as an administrator.

## Who does this step

The school or club normally handles the federation-side approval.

The dancer verifies that the result is in place before entering regulated workflows.

## What the dancer should ask the school or club

1. Is the club already approved in the federation?
2. Does my license use the same federation?
3. Do I need any club-specific approval before I can register?
4. Is there any age, class, or representation issue that should be solved first?

## How it works

1. Confirm the correct school or club is connected to the right federation.
2. Confirm any required club or organization relationship.
3. Confirm the correct license is active.
4. Make sure the federation-specific requirements are satisfied before entering events.

## What to remember

A dancer may feel ready to compete before the federation state is fully ready. Those are not always the same thing.

## Common blockers

- The school or club is not properly approved in the federation workflow.
- The dancer has a club but no valid federation-linked license.
- The dancer assumes one federation path works for another.
- The dancer tries to register before federation-related approvals are finished.

## What success looks like

The dancer can move forward into event registration without federation eligibility surprises, and the school or club has already handled the federation-side joining step.

## If you are still blocked

If you have a valid account and a valid club relationship but still cannot continue, the blocker is usually one of these:

- the club is not yet approved by the federation
- the wrong federation was chosen for the license
- the federation requires approval that the club has not completed yet
