# Dancer retiring

Retiring usually means stepping away from active competition while keeping historical records intact.

## What this step is for

This step helps dancers end active participation cleanly without leaving confusion for the club, organizer, or federation.

## What retiring does not mean

It does not usually mean deleting the account or wiping historical results.

It usually means stopping active competition use while keeping records understandable.

## How it works

1. Finish or cancel remaining event plans.
2. Inform your club or team if your membership context will change.
3. Check whether any pending registrations or license actions should be closed first.
4. Stop renewing competition-related status when you no longer need it.
5. Keep historical rankings and event records intact for reference.

## Good practice

- Resolve open registrations before retiring.
- Let your organization know if team composition will be affected.
- Keep one primary account rather than trying to start over with a new account later.

## What success looks like

The dancer stops active competition participation cleanly, while old records remain understandable and traceable.
