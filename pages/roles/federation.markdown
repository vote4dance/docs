# Federation

**You are a federation administrator if** you oversee a national, regional, or local governing body that sets policies, issues licenses, manages rankings, and approves schools and clubs to participate.

This role hub covers:
- Setting up federation governance and admins
- Defining license requirements and items
- Creating license pricing and approval workflows
- Managing schools/clubs and their eligibility
- Creating and managing rankings
- Setting policy for transfers and eligibility

**Not sure if you're a federation admin?** Start with [Finding Your Role](/understanding-vote4dance/finding-your-role/).

**Just starting out?** Most federation setup is better handled from the dedicated feature sections below.

## How to use this hub

1. Start on a federation-owned step if you are deciding policy or governance.
2. Switch to [Federation Licenses](/federation-licenses/) when you are doing actual license setup or approval work.
3. Switch to [Federation Rankings](/federation-rankings/) when you are doing actual ranking setup, manual exceptions, or public verification.
4. Use [Release notes](/release-notes/) only for product-change awareness, not as workflow guidance.
