# Federation school or club application model

For the federation, this step defines how schools and clubs enter the federation-managed workflow.

## What this step is for

This step describes how organizations are approved, reviewed, and allowed to act inside federation rules.

## How it works

1. Define what an organization must provide.
2. Decide who reviews organization requests.
3. Decide whether organizations may issue or approve licenses.
4. Approve only organizations that meet the federation standard.
5. Keep approvals reviewable and consistent.

## Why this matters

Schools and clubs are often the operational bridge between dancers and federation policy.

## Common blockers

- Organization requirements are unclear.
- Approval rights are granted too widely.
- Organizations assume approval in one area means approval everywhere.

## What success looks like

The federation has a predictable organization onboarding model that supports the wider season workflow.
